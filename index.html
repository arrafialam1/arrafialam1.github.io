About Me

A self-motivated and research-driven candidate with a background in Nuclear Science & Engineering and extensive experience in data analysis, programming, and technical research. My early fascination with cricket statistics ignited a passion for analytical thinking that flourished during my undergraduate studies. I co-authored research papers on innovative solutions for nuclear facilities—including work on wireless safety systems—that underscored the profound potential of data analysis and programming in tackling complex challenges. I presented this work at the 2019 ICREST in Dhaka and the paper was later published in IEEE Xplore. Managing operational data for our family’s business further revealed the transformative power of data-driven decision-making and encouraged me to explore AI and Data Science. Through hands-on projects, I developed proficiency in Python, SQL, and Power BI, reinforcing my belief in the capacity of these tools to drive meaningful global change. Motivated by a passion for innovation and societal impact, I am eager to pursue a Master's in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science to deepen my expertise and contribute to cutting-edge, ethical AI solutions for pressing global challenges.
